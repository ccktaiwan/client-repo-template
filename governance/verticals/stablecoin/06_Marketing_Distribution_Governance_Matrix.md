Purpose and Scope
This document defines enforceable governance controls for all marketing,
communication, and distribution activities related to the Stablecoin
vertical under the client repository template. It governs messaging,
channel usage, audience targeting, incentive mechanisms, and public
disclosures prior to client repository instantiation.

This matrix applies to all internal and external communication channels,
distribution platforms, and promotional mechanisms associated with
Stablecoin operations.

Governed Marketing and Distribution Domains
The following domains are governed.

- Public marketing communications.

- Distribution channels and platforms.

- Incentive and referral programs.

- Partner and affiliate communications.

- Public disclosures and announcements.

Marketing and Distribution Principles
The following principles are mandatory.

- Communications MUST be accurate and non-misleading.

- Distribution activities MUST be authorized and traceable.

- Incentives MUST NOT distort economic reality.

- All public disclosures MUST be auditable.

Marketing and Distribution Lifecycle
All activities are governed across the following stages.

- Planning.

- Content creation.

- Review and approval.

- Distribution execution.

- Monitoring and reporting.

Mandatory Marketing and Distribution Rules
The following rules are enforceable.

- Marketing content MUST NOT be published without governance approval.

- Claims regarding value, stability, or guarantees are PROHIBITED.

- Distribution through unauthorized channels is PROHIBITED.

- Incentive programs MUST be explicitly approved.

- Retroactive modification of public communications is PROHIBITED.

Constraint Definitions

- Accuracy Constraint  
All communications MUST accurately reflect system behavior and risks.

- Channel Constraint  
Only governance-approved channels MAY be used.

- Incentive Constraint  
Incentives MUST NOT exceed approved parameters.

- Disclosure Constraint  
Mandatory disclosures MUST accompany relevant communications.

Interfaces and Boundaries

- Content Submission Interface  
Accepts marketing materials with declared scope and intent.

- Approval Interface  
Issues approval records for publication.

- Distribution Interface  
Executes publication through authorized channels only.

- Monitoring Interface  
Collects distribution metrics and compliance signals.

Enforcement and Blocking Logic

- Unauthorized publication MUST be blocked.

- Non-compliant content MUST be rejected.

- Violations MUST generate governance incidents.

- Repeat violations MUST trigger access suspension.

Audit and Traceability

- All communications MUST be traceable to approval records.

- Distribution logs MUST be immutable.

- Audit systems MUST reconcile published content against approvals.

Change Management

- Changes MUST follow Prospera OS Level A decision procedures.

- Unauthorized changes are INVALID.
