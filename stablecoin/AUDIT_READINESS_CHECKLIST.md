Purpose

This checklist defines the mandatory audit readiness requirements
for stablecoin client repositories operating under Prospera OS governance.

It ensures that all engineering, data usage, AI-assisted activities,
and decision pathways remain verifiable, attributable, and compliant.

---

Audit Scope

This checklist applies to:

- Stablecoin system implementation
- Smart contract development
- Data access and usage
- AI-assisted engineering activities
- Governance adoption and compliance artifacts
- Marketing and distribution implementations

---

Governance Adoption Verification

- Governance adoption declaration is present and unmodified
- Governance source repository is correctly referenced
- No governance rules are redefined locally
- Decision chains are referenced, not duplicated

---

Decision Chain Compliance

- All critical actions map to a defined decision chain
- Block points and escalation paths are documented
- Human approval points are explicit
- No direct execution bypass exists

---

AI Usage Compliance

- AI usage rules document is present
- AI is restricted to engineering worker role
- All AI outputs are logged and traceable
- Human review is documented for AI-generated artifacts
- No autonomous AI execution is present

---

Data Usage and Privacy Controls

- Data access scopes are defined and enforced
- Customer data handling follows decision chain rules
- Logging exists for data access and transformation
- No undocumented data replication or export exists

---

Engineering Traceability

- All commits are attributable to a human maintainer
- Commit messages reflect intent and scope
- Changes are reviewable and reproducible
- Release artifacts are versioned

---

Security and Access Control

- Access permissions follow least-privilege principles
- Secrets are not committed to the repository
- Deployment credentials are segregated
- Emergency access paths are documented

---

External Dependency Review

- Third-party services are documented
- Dependency versions are pinned
- License compatibility is reviewed
- External APIs are approved by governance scope

---

Audit Evidence Availability

- Logs are retained according to policy
- Decision records are retrievable
- AI interaction records are accessible
- Compliance documents are current

---

Non-Compliance Handling

- Violations are documented
- Escalation procedures are defined
- Remediation actions are tracked
- Governance notification procedures are followed

---

Canonical Statement

Audit readiness is continuous.

Compliance is not optional.
